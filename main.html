Darjeeling, India


Darjeeling, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, is known for its breathtaking views of Mount Kanchenjunga, lush tea plantations, and colonial charm. Often referred to as the "Queen of the Hills," it is a perfect blend of natural beauty, culture, and adventure.

  1. Darjeeling Himalayan Railway – Take a ride on the famous Toy Train, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  2. Tiger Hill – Experience the magical sunrise over Mount Kanchenjunga.
  3. Batasia Loop – A picturesque spot offering panoramic views of the mountains.
  4. Peace Pagoda – A serene monument symbolizing peace and tranquility.
  5. Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park – Explore the rich wildlife, including the endangered Red Panda.
  • Try the world-renowned Darjeeling Tea, known for its exquisite flavor and aroma.
  • Momos – A popular Tibetan-style dumpling, available with both vegetarian and meat fillings.
  • Enjoy a warm bowl of Thukpa, a traditional Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Shaphaley – A Tibetan deep-fried meat pie with a crispy exterior and flavorful filling.
  • Indulge in Sel Roti, a homemade, ring-shaped rice bread, often served during festivals.
